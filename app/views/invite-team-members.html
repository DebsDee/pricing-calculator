Organisation roles

Which tasks can they perform?

Org manager

A superuser who can create and delete spaces within an org, and administer user roles, both within the org and its spaces. We recommend to have at least two org managers.

Org auditor

A user who can view quota allocation and user roles across the org. Auditors can't change any of these configurations.

Org billing manager

A user who can see billing, costs and quota information.

Tasks related to spaces are associated with space-level roles.

Space roles

Space manager

A superuser who manages the space and its users.

Space developer

A user who can deploy, run and manage apps, instantiate backing services and bind them to apps.

Space auditor

A user who can view apps, users and resources used within the space.
