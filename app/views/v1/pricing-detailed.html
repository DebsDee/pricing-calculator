Pricing

Start with a free trial account

You can test the platform for up to three months, with no obligations to pay anything while you decide whether GOV.UK PaaS is for you. You’ll get to use trial-sized editions of most of our backing services.

Request a free trial account

Move to a full account with no long-term contract

During the trial period you can switch to a full paid account any time and get to use all of the functionality of the PaaS.

You don’t have to commit to a long-term contract, and you can enable and disable apps, databases and services as required.

The monthly charges will be based on your usage.

Costs and benefits of a PaaS

If you’re evaluating cloud hosting, you’ll find that the costs of a PaaS are higher than a traditional IaaS solution. This is also true for GOV.UK PaaS.

In order to compare the two, you need to consider the effort and time required by your team to manage and run the environments, like patching operating systems, managing backups, maintaining logging stacks, monitoring the infrastructure and reprovisioning your apps.

Service team support plans

While we’re responsible for the overall health of the platform, your service might need help using the PaaS, and support for specific issues caused by the platform to your app.

There is a minimum monthly fee that depends on whether support is needed 24/7 or just during office hours, and the final cost is calculated on how many apps, instances and backing services you are using. We won’t charge users in trial mode for support, however resolution times will depend on best endeavours. You remain responsible for product and end user support.
